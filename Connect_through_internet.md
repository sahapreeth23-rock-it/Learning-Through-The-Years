# Internet addresses
There are two addressing systems_ Number addressing system and letter addressing system:
a)Number addressing system: The address is composed of numbers,It has 4 numbers, each in range 0 to 255, joined together by period.
ex: 192.12.148.73 and 131.58.97.254 (IP address)
b)Letter addressing system: The address is composed of letter of alphabet, also called as *DOMAIN NAME SYSTEM* 
ex: cbse.nic.in and india.oup.com
The letter addressing system also uses two letter country codes, like
ex:com Commercial organization
   gov Government department
   ca Canada
   de Germany
